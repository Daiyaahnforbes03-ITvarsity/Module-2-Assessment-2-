INCEPTION

STARRING: Leonardo DiCaprio, Ken Watanabe, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Marion Cotillard, Elliot Page, Tom Hardy, Cillian Murphy, Tom Berenger, Michael Caine

AGE RESTRICTION: PG-13

SHOWING TIMES: 2:00 PM, 5:30 PM, 8:15 PM

SYNOPSIS: Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, is a master in the art of "extraction," stealing secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Now he is given a final task that may grant him his life back, but it's a job that requires him to plant an idea into someone's mind.
