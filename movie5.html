THE SHAWSHANK REDEMTION

STARRING: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman, Bob Gunton, William Sadler, Clancy Brown, Gil Bellows, James Whitmore,

AGE RESTRICTION: R

SHOWING TIMES: 2:30 PM, 5:15 PM, 8:45 PM

SYNOPSIS: Framed for the murder of his wife, banker Andy Dufresne is sentenced to Shawshank State Penitentiary. Over two decades, he forms a deep friendship with fellow inmate Red and works tirelessly to prove his innocence.
