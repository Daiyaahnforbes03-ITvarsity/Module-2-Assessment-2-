LA LA LAND

STARRING: Ryan Gosling, Emma Stone, John Legend, Rosemarie DeWitt, Finn Wittrock, J. K. Simmons,

AGE RESTRICTION: PG-13

SHOWING TIMES: 1:30 PM, 4:45 PM, 8:00 PM
