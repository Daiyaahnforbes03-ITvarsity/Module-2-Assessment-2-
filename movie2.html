THE GRAND BUDAPEST HOTEL

STARRING: Ralph Fiennes, F. Murray Abraham, Mathieu Amalric, Adrien Brody, Willem Dafoe, Jeff Goldblum, Harvey Keitel, Jude Law, Bill Murray, Edward Norton, Saoirse Ronan, Jason Schwartzman, Léa Seydoux, Tilda Swinton, Tom Wilkinson, Owen Wilson, Tony Revolori,

AGE RESTRICTION: R

SHOWING TIMES: 1:45 PM, 4:30 PM, 7:00 PM

SYNOPSIS: This comedic caper follows the misadventures of Gustave H., a legendary concierge, and his protégé, Zero, at the Grand Budapest Hotel. The two become embroiled in the theft and recovery of a priceless Renaissance painting and the battle for an enormous family fortune.
